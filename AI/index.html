Dashboard Guide

What is this?
The AI Pulse Dashboard is a centralized status monitor for various Artificial Intelligence API providers. It tracks availability, supported features, and model lists for major providers like OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, and many others.
How to Test an API
  • Step 1: Find a provider in the list below. Use the column headers to sort by name or status.
  • Step 2: Click the "Test" button on the right side of the row.
  • Step 3: In the popup, select the specific Model you want to query.
  • Step 4: Enter your own API Key.
    (Note: Your key is processed locally in your browser to send the request. It is NOT saved or sent to our server.)
  • Step 5: Click "Run Test" to verify if the model is online and responding.
