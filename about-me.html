About Me

Hello!

I'm Tony Nahra, a passionate Software Engineer and Data Professional with a deep love for solving complex problems through technology. My journey in tech started over two decades ago, fueled by a curiosity for how things work—from the logic of code to the physics of the universe.

My Philosophy

I believe that technology is most powerful when it bridges the gap between data and human understanding. Whether I'm designing a database architecture, building an interactive tutorial engine, or analyzing financial trends, my goal is always clarity, efficiency, and usability. I don't just write code; I build systems that empower users.

Professional Journey

Over the years, I've had the privilege of working in diverse sectors, including healthcare and finance. At the Patient Education Institute, I led the development of multimedia tutorial systems that have educated millions of patients worldwide. My work involved not just coding, but architecting scalable solutions that could handle multilingual content, offline access via tablets, and seamless integration with hospital EMR systems like Cerner.

Prior to that, at the Abu Dhabi Investment Authority, I honed my skills in data analysis and financial modeling. I developed tools to simulate investment strategies, back-test theories, and visualize complex financial data for analysts managing billions in assets.
