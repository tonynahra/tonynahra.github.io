About Me

Hello, I'm Tony. Welcome to my personal page. I'm a technology professional driven by a lifelong passion for solving complex problems. For me, programming, software, and mathematics were hobbies long before they became a career, which has allowed me to build a broad and deep set of technical skills over the years.

My professional background spans over 25 years, with a primary focus on the healthcare and financial sectors. I specialize in database architecture, statistical analysis, and data warehousing. Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of leading international technical teams in architecting and developing robust, full-stack systems. This work has included building everything from secure financial data-extraction tools to multilingual, SCORM-compliant learning platforms.

I hold a Bachelor of Engineering degree and have gathered a wide range of certifications and senior-level experience. I am committed to building efficient, scalable systems and am always exploring new horizons in technology, from cloud infrastructure and AI to the fundamentals of signal processing.

When I'm not coding or thinking about technology, my interests are quite diverse. I have a deep appreciation for finance, investment strategies, and the logic of chess. I also enjoy classical music, mathematics, and physics. I love spending time in nature and am incredibly proud that my passion for technology has passed on to my three children, all of whom are computer engineers.
