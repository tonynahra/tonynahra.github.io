X-Plain Health Encyclopedia for Websites

The X-Plain Health Encyclopedia for Websites is designed for hospital websites and health systems. It includes our entire library (+10,000 tutorials, illustrated handouts, and videos), covering all medical specialties. ​

Nurses can print branded handouts, display the X-Plain videos on bedside TVs, run the tutorials on tablets, or send assignments electronically for patients to complete at home. Patients, caregivers, and health consumers visiting the website can search for specific health topics and learn in the format they choose: video, tutorial, text, or illustrated PDF.

This solution is the easiest to implement and can be used anywhere there is internet access, whether at the point of care or beyond. This solution can also increase website traffic and attract new patients. ​
The subscription fee, which includes our standard services, is based on the number of hospital beds and other metrics for other institutions.
