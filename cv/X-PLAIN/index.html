X-Plain Patient Education - For a Better Healthcare Experience

We implement patient education and engagement solutions that meet critical clients’ needs. We offer content with services that include automated updates, branding with the client's name, integration with existing healthcare IT systems, evaluation, and best-practice dissemination. We are the Patient Education Institute, publisher of "X-Plain Patient Education" or simply X-Plain.
​ X-Plain content includes thousands of evidence-based, peer-reviewed titles in several languages and three educational formats: videos, illustrated text, and tutorials.
We develop apps and webservices for tablets, desktops, and websites. We also integrate with the software of third-party vendors for EMRs, patient portals, bedside patient stations, and other healthcare IT innovations.
